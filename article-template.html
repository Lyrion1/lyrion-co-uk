Ritual & Intention: Setting Your Monthly Moon Cycle

Published: January 2025 | Category: Astrology

The moon pulls the tides and it pulls our intention. To live in alignment with the monthly lunar cycle is to embrace the cosmic rhythm of growth and release. The New Moon is for planting seeds—setting quiet, powerful intentions. The Full Moon is for illumination—a time to celebrate what has bloomed and to cut cords with what no longer serves the soul's path. This isn't just theory; it is practical magic.

LYRĪON couture is built upon this cyclical understanding. Our pieces are designed not just to be worn, but to become tactile artifacts in your personal ritual. Imagine the "Zenith Bodysuit" worn specifically during the New Moon phase as a second skin for your freshest intentions. The power of the garment is in the intention you assign it.

By connecting your wardrobe choices to the celestial calendar, you transform everyday dressing into an act of profound self-acknowledgment. This integration of the astronomical and the corporeal is the essence of living with ritual. Start small: note the phase, set a single intention, and choose one garment that embodies that purpose. The cosmos awaits your conscious participation.
